Our Mission

GEOClockIn®’s mission is to empower organizations with the ability to take greater control of their business by leveraging new GPS and biometric technologies. This radically aggressive new smartphone technology arms organizations with the ability to better track their employees and their activities.

Through the GPS tracking technologies, the GEOClockIn® app dramatically reduces the amount of time needed to check whether the employees are on the premises and doing what they are supposed to do.

Our Goals
  • Empower organization's employing hourly working using time-clocks to keep track of their employees hours with a unique way to verify the truthfulness of same.
  • Allow employers to see the amount of time their employees are spending smoking cigarettes or taking breaks vs. working.
  • Arm employers with the ability to recognize and reward those employees who are showing up to work on time, working their allotted time during the day.
  • Provide a system to identify those workers who are a) showing up to work late, b) taking too long of a lunch break and c) leaving early. Provide a notification warning system to ensure compliance.
  • Allow senior management the capability to see what the company is losing in terms of salaries for employees showing up to work late, taking too long of a lunch break, smoking at work and leaving early.

GeoClockIn® was able to determine these fraudulent activities were costing us over twenty thousand dollars each week.
